Our Mission

At Mainland Consultants, our aim is to provide you with Construction Management solutions, which are tailored to your individual needs.

Property development projects albeit large or small can bedaunting. Through our knowledge and experience we will guide you through the whole process and ensure your aspiriations are met to the highest standards.

Services

Investment

Mainland helps companies, funders and investors, wisely bid for, and unlock developement opportunities within competitive property markets. With the experience and expertise of our people and cutting-edge market intelligence; we can help ensure that your development vision is turned into reality.


Understanding local property values, the strength of rental demands and prices, other local investment opportunities, transport routes, local amenities and much more. This knowledge in turn means our investors can feel assured their investment coupled with our recommedation - is safe and secure, and will yield continued profits over short and long term.

  • Identifying potential opportunities
  • Match prospective investors
  • Mortgage advice & Legal support
  • Asset Handover and if required resale

Design

Mainland Consultants provide design solutions: Architectural, and Structal plans to suit project requirements. Whether you build from new, refit or completely refurbish.


You may have clear or unclear ideas of what you want done to your asset, which we will discuss in detail during our initial consultation. Our team will help create a space that inspires you, by listening to, and understanding your needs.

  • Initial Consultation
  • Site Survey and Investigation
  • Detail Design
  • Planning & Building Regulation approvals

Construction

With our construction partners and supply chain, we are able to construct and refurbish for both public and private sector clients.


Whether its creating a new buliding - or breathing life into an existing one. We aspire to make reality a vision that is shared by both the client, and the designer. Resulting in a construction that is befitting to the people who will use it.

  • Tender & Contract procurement
  • Project Management
  • Completion certificate and Health and Safety File

Portfolio Management

As part of our services here at Mainland we offer portfolio management. This service extends to our clients who have had more than one property developed by us.

  • Portfolio review
  • Refinancing
  • Property Management
